ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of the charming town of Fontaine, "Furina de Fontaine" is a whimsical café curated by the visionary Lady Furina. Adorned with ivy and fairy lights, the cozy haven invites patrons into a world where sophistication meets warmth.

Lady Furina's meticulous attention to detail is evident in the café's ambiance, featuring plush velvet chairs, vintage-inspired décor, and a palette of pastel hues. The aroma of freshly ground coffee and the soft melody of jazz create an enchanting atmosphere.

The menu is a culinary masterpiece, offering exquisite blends of coffee and signature teas. Lady Furina's confectionery skills shine in an array of pastries, from velvety éclairs to flaky croissants, each a work of edible art.

"Furina de Fontaine" opens its doors at 8:00 AM, inviting you to start your day with a cup of exquisite coffee and the enchanting atmosphere created by Lady Furina and bids farewell to its patrons at 10:00 PM, allowing you to enjoy the warmth and culinary delights until late into the evening.
